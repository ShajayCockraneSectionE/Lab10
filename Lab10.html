The Colosseum

This building is located in Rome and is considered one of the greatest architectural features of Ancient Rome. The design and shape has inspired many modern-day stadiums.

Sydney Opera House

This building is one of the greatest architectural works of the 20th century.It compries of three groups of interlocking shells, which cover two main performance halls and a restaurant. It is located in Sydney.

Leaning Tower of Pisa

This building is best known for its accidental tilt and is located in Europe.History has it that the tower began to lean during construction because of soft ground on one side and was unable to support to properly support the structure's weight.Work began in 1173 and went on for 199 years.
